Mandate

The Department of Education was established through the Education Decree of 1863 as the Superior Commission Primary Instruction under a Chairman. The Education agency underwent many recognition efforts in the 20th century to better define its purpose via a vis the changing administrations and charters. The present-day Department of Education was eventually mandated through Republic Act 9155, otherwise known as the Governance of Basic Education Act of 2001, which established the mandate of this agency. The Department of Education (DepEd) formulates, implements, and coordinates policies, plans, programs, and projects in the areas of formal and non-formal basic education. It supervises all elementary and secondary education institutions, including alternative learning systems, both public and private; and provides for the establishment and maintenance of a complete, adequate, and integrated system of basic education relevant to the goals of national development

Vision

The City of Bacoor National High School-Springville envisions Bacooreño learners who possess the value of patriotism, a sound value system, skills, and competencies that enable them towards self-actualization to contribute significantly in building a better community. As a government institution under the Department of Education, which is a learner-centered public institution, City of Bacoor National High School-Springville commits itself to academic advancement and character building to better serve its stakeholders.

Mission

The City of Bacoor National High School-Springville is committed to safeguard and nurture the right of every Bacooreño Learner to excellent, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education. Where every learner acquires knowledge in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment facilitated and nurtured by teachers. Ensuring an enabling and supportive environment manned by the administration and staff for well-organized learning to transpire. Thereby, family, community, and other stakeholders are expected to build alliances and shared responsibility to equip every learner with essential knowledge, skills, and a sound value system.
